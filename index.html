About Us

We are FilantroPH and we dream of a better community





Who are we?


     FilantroPH is a disruptive Filipino startup social enterprise that consists of a team of passionate individuals who share the same passion in uplifting the plight of the poor and the needy. We believe that social responsibility and entrepreneurship can go hand-in-hand in solving various social issues in the country such as poverty, illiteracy, unemployment and malnutrition.


From left to right: Virgil Francis Puray (Co-founder), Graham Paul Jimenez (Co-founder), Athi Selvanayagam (1337 Ventures), John Benedict Bernardo (Technical Mentor)








mission


     Our mission is to connect charities to individuals who love to read heart-touching stories, individuals who want to change and uplift the status of the country and individuals who want to be shaped with values through the spirit of volunteerism.


vision


     Social Responsibility and Unselfish Dedication are two of FilantroPH's core values. By these core values, we envision a society where suffering and hunger is cured, a society where the environment is preserved, a society that upholds the spirit of philanthropy and generosity and lastly, a society of peace and equality for every Filipino. This is FilantroPH's vision.




goal


     FilantroPH's aims to provide digital presence for all legitimate charities in the country. By providing a platform for charitable organizations wherein they can post about themselves, their updates, success stories, accomplishments, needs, causes, activities and use volunteer gathering tools without worrying costs and security, we can help charities radiate their impact to people from all parts of the world.
